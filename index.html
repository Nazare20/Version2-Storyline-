Version 2

Goal:

Get your Chicken Nugget To The Ship Before It's Too Late

Storyline:

WW5 just ended and all of earth is in ruins. A young chicken nugget named Bob was launched into space from the radioactive shockwave and is fighting for his life to survive. Bob needs to get to a ship where all of his chicken nugget relatives are located. Get Bob to the ship before it's too late...

Rules:

  • Player Moving Commands:
  • W | UP
  • A | LEFT
  • S | DOWN
  • D | RIGHT

  • Shields (Unlocked after 2400 metres)
  • Left Arrow Key | Left Shield
  • Right Arrow Key | Right Shield
  • Up Arrow Key | Top Shield
  • Down Arrow Key | Bottom Shield
  • The map teleports so if you are at
    the very left of the map and move
    the player left it will warp
    you to the left side. If you are at the very right and move the player
    right it will warp you to the
    left side. This applies for all sides.

  • Shooter Commands (Unlocked after 2400 metres)
  • You can shoot with your mouse
    (A helpful dot will show up to help you aim)
    If this dot does not show up try moving your
    cursor a bit away from the player
  • Reload Bullets: R

  • Ship
  • You get to the ship after 4400 metres

  • Problems
  • If your controls aren't working
    try clicking on the screen
    that's running your game again
